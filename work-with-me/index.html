How we can work together

Each engagement is scoped to what you actually need. Here's what I typically do:

FHIR Training

Workshops · 2-5 days · Fixed price

Hands-on training covering FHIR fundamentals through advanced profiling and implementation guide development. I tailor the content to your team's level - whether they're starting from scratch or need to deepen existing skills in areas like resource modelling, terminology binding, or search.

For teams adopting FHIR who need structured, practical training to get productive fast.

Add-on

+ AI for FHIR

Additional 1-2 days · Fixed price

Extend your FHIR training with AI-accelerated workflows. Your team learns to leverage AI for profiling, validation, concept mapping, and implementation - in a way that maintains quality, standards compliance, and safety. Not "here's ChatGPT, good luck" but structured methods for using AI as a real force multiplier.

For teams who want to combine FHIR proficiency with modern AI tools - safely and with quality.

Implementation Consulting

Embedded · Flexible scope · Weekly rate

Architecture design, implementation guide development, clinical concept mapping (SNOMED CT, LOINC, ICD-10, ICD-11, and more), and conformance testing. I've done this for EHR vendors, national agencies, and EU research projects across the Nordics, Netherlands, Belgium, Israel, US, and Australia.

For organizations building FHIR-based systems who need an architect who's seen what works across multiple countries.

Conformance Audit

Fixed scope · 1-2 weeks · Fixed price

An independent review of your FHIR implementation for conformance, interoperability, and architectural risks. You get a prioritized report - what needs fixing, why, and how.

For teams approaching go-live, certification, or a partner integration who want confidence their implementation holds up.

Technical Advisory

Retainer · A few hours per week

Ongoing access to a seasoned FHIR expert for architecture decisions, standards strategy, terminology questions, and the hard problems that come up between milestones.

For teams who don't need full-time help but need fast, correct answers to hard questions.

Board Membership

Ongoing · Governance

Domain expertise and an international network at the governance level. As a current HL7 Sweden board member, I understand standards governance from the inside. I help health tech companies and standards bodies navigate regulatory landscapes, standards adoption, and market strategy.

For health tech companies and standards organizations looking for independent expertise and cross-market perspective at the board level.
